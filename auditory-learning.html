Verbal Mnemonics for HTML

Document Structure

"Hungry Hippos Bring Lunch"

  • H - HTML tag
  • H - Head section
  • B - Body section
  • L - Links and content

Essential Elements

"SHIP"

  • S - Structure (headings)
  • H - Hyperlinks
  • I - Images
  • P - Paragraphs
